Our Total Rewards

We care for our employees by offering competitive benefits in Maloti (M) and a supportive work environment.

Health & Wellness

  • Comprehensive medical aid (options: network & private)
  • Dental & vision coverage
  • Wellness allowance: M1,500 per annum (gym, mental health)
  • Annual health screenings

Leave & Time Off

  • 20 days annual leave + 5 family responsibility days
  • 12 paid public holidays (Lesotho calendar)
  • Maternity / Paternity: 4 months / 10 days fully paid
  • Study leave: up to 10 days/year for approved programs

Allowances & Financial

  • Transport allowance: M2,200/month
  • 13th cheque (performance-based bonus)
  • Cellphone & data reimbursement: M500/month
  • Pension fund: 7.5% employer contribution

Professional Development

  • Annual training budget: up to M12,000 per employee
  • Certification sponsorships (HR, Leadership, Tech)
  • Mentorship program & career coaching

Additional Perks

  • Flexible hybrid work model (remote up to 2 days/week)
  • Employee assistance program (counseling, legal advice)
  • Team building retreats & annual family day

All monetary values are in Lesotho Maloti (M).
